Frequently Asked Questions

How does Talent Lens AI screening work?

Our AI analyzes candidate responses through video interviews, evaluating communication skills, technical knowledge, and cultural fit. The system uses natural language processing and machine learning to provide detailed insights about each candidate.

What is the pricing for Talent Lens services?

We offer flexible pricing plans based on your company size and needs. Contact our sales team for a customized quote that fits your recruitment requirements and budget.

How long does the setup process take?

The setup process typically takes 24-48 hours. Our team will guide you through the configuration, integration with your existing systems, and provide training for your HR team.

Is my data secure with Talent Lens?

Yes, we prioritize data security with enterprise-grade encryption, secure cloud storage, and compliance with international data protection regulations including GDPR and CCPA.

Can I integrate Talent Lens with my existing HR tools?

Talent Lens integrates seamlessly with popular HR platforms, ATS systems, and recruitment tools. Our API allows for custom integrations as well.
